# Data Products

A data product is a supported, discoverable, and contractually defined dataset or service—not simply any table in the gold layer.

Every data product must define:

- Business problem and intended consumers
- Owner and support team
- Contracted schema and semantic definitions
- Grain, keys, and relationships
- Freshness, availability, and quality objectives
- Classification and permitted uses
- Upstream dependencies and downstream consumers
- Versioning and deprecation policy
- Cost and operational health measures

The product catalog will distinguish BI products, operational products, data science products, AI products, and API products.
